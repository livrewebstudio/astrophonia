Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2025

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They can remember your preferences and choices, making your experience smoother.

Cookies We Use

AstroPhonia uses only essential cookies. These are strictly necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They include:

  • ap_lang — Remembers your chosen language (EN / IT / PT). Expires: session / local storage (no expiry, clears with browser data).
  • ap_cookies — Records that you have acknowledged this cookie notice. Expires: local storage.

Both values are stored in localStorage (not traditional cookies), meaning they are never transmitted to any server and remain only on your device.

What We Do NOT Use

We do not use:

  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Analytics tools (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Social media pixels or trackers
  • Any third-party cookie scripts

Third-Party Platforms

When you leave this site to visit Bandcamp, their own cookie and privacy policies apply. We have no control over their cookies. You can review their policy at bandcamp.com/privacy.

How to Control Cookies

You can delete or block localStorage data through your browser settings at any time. Doing so will reset your language preference and cookie acknowledgement. Most browsers also offer "private" or "incognito" modes that do not retain localStorage data.

Contact

For any questions about our cookie use: contato@astrophonia.com

O que são Cookies?

Cookies são pequenos arquivos de texto armazenados no seu dispositivo pelo navegador ao visitar um site. Eles podem lembrar suas preferências e escolhas, tornando sua experiência mais fluida.
